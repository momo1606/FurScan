About Us

Welcome to Furscan! We understand the deep bond between you and your four-legged companion, and we're dedicated to ensuring their health and happiness.

Our platform serves as a bridge between devoted dog owners and experienced veterinarians, focusing specifically on canine skin health. With Furscan, you have the power to take control of your furry friend's dermatological concerns like never before.

How does it work? It's simple. Upload a photo of your dog's skin issue, and our advanced AI swiftly identifies the problem, generating a detailed report. But that's not where our journey ends. This report is then carefully reviewed by a qualified veterinarian.

We believe that every dog deserves the best care possible, and we're here to make that a reality. Let's embark on a journey towards a happier, healthier life for your beloved pet.

Our Mission

Empowering dog owners with knowledge and connecting them with expert veterinarians for accurate diagnosis and compassionate care of their furry companions' skin issues.

To create a global community where every dog receives timely and accurate skin health assessments, leading to happier, healthier lives.

Furscan combines cutting-edge AI technology with the expertise of trusted veterinarians, revolutionizing the way we understand and address dog skin diseases.
