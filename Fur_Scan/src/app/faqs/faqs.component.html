FAQ's


Q: How does Furscan work?

Furscan uses advanced AI technology to analyze uploaded images of your dog's skin issues. The AI provides an initial diagnosis, which is then verified by a qualified veterinarian to ensure accuracy.

Q: What types of skin issues can Furscan identify?

Furscan can help identify a wide range of common skin conditions in dogs, including allergies, infections, dermatitis, parasites, and more


Q: Is Furscan a substitute for visiting a physical vet?

Furscan is a complementary tool, designed to provide initial insights. However, it's important to consult with a local veterinarian for comprehensive care and treatment options.

Q: How do I upload images of my dog's skin issue?

You can easily upload images through our user-friendly interface. Simply follow the prompts on the website to attach clear and well-lit photos of the affected area.


Q: How long does it take to receive a diagnosis report?

The initial AI analysis is typically completed within minutes. After that, our team of veterinarians will review and verify the report, aiming to provide you with a final assessment as soon as possible.

Q: Is Furscan's AI technology reliable?

Yes, our AI is trained on a vast dataset of canine skin images and is continuously updated with the latest information to ensure accurate diagnoses.


Q: Is my uploaded data secure and private?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All information you provide is treated with strict confidentiality and is only accessible to authorized personnel for the purpose of diagnosis.
